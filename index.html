Our Scope of
Services

Our Scope of services growing with each passing day, in terms of coverage, are ensuring that the best treatment is provided to every patient utilizing them. Here’s a list of the services that the Hospital is currently working with.

Emergency & ICU Services

Fully equipped with the state's best and most responsive team of dedicated Emergency Medicine Doctors.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

The department boasts of the best doctors with decades of experience in their fields with all latest equipment and dedicated ICUs..

X-Ray, CT Scan, Ultrasound, MRI

128 Slice CT-Angio, Fully Digital 1000 ma X-Ray, Portable and Fixed High Res GE Ultrasound, MRI

ECG, Echo, TMT

The most advanced machines and a state of the art facility housing them, coupled with dedicated doctors make for a quick test and even quicker reports.

Orthopaedics Surgery & Joint Replacement

The department boasts of full-fledged Ortho Services including complex surgeries related to Joint Replacement and Spine Treatment.

General Physician

Out Patient and In-Patient services are covered with experienced Specialists housing decades of experience.

Pathology Lab Services

Fully Digitized Pathology Lab Services including Bio-Chemistry and Microbiological investigations, all conducted in-house with a complete digitized reporting system making for quick investigations and convenient report access.

Pediatrics & Neonatology

Equipped with a state of art Paediatric ICU and Neonatal ICU, and highly specialized doctors, the hospital is very well equipped to handle all emergencies and treatments related to infants and young kids.

ENT

Out Patient and In-Patient services are covered with experienced Specialists housing decades of experience.

Pulmonology

With renowned specialists onboard, the Pulmonology Department at GEIMS is one that competes with any other department on the national or international level.

Physiotherapy

Outpatient and In-Patient services are covered multiple qualified and trained Physiotherapists available round the clock.

Pharmacy

A fully equipped and digitized IP and OP Pharmacy operational 24x7, always fulfilling all medication requirements.

General Surgery

Top General Surgery consultants are constantly attending to the needs of patients coming in with requirements of simple to extremely complex surgeries

Dietetics

Well qualified dieticians are available round the clock, to ensure proper nutritional guidance to all admitted patients as well as specific requests by OPD attendees.

Dental

A full-fledged Dental Department, consisting of the Top Dental Surgeons of the country, caters to all types of dental services requirements in both OP and IP mode.

Neurosurgery

The department being handled by one of the Top Neurosurgery Consultants of the region, is actively contributing to timely treatment of Neuro Patients from Emergencies to OutPatient visits.

Ophthalmology

The Department being headed by an extremely experienced Consultant, is well equipped to handle all OP, IP and DayCare procedures related to eye care.

Gastroenterology

Arguably, the best Gastro Department in the state under the leadership of one of India’s Top Gastro Consultants, the department is fully operational with IP and OP Services available with simple to very complex surgeries being done on a regular basis.

Anaesthesiology

A big team of in house anaesthetists are present round the clock, to ensure all surgeries and other procedures are well taken care of, along with handling OP Patients as a part of Pain Clinic.

Dialysis

A full fledged Dialysis Centre handled by Experienced Physicians and a visiting Nephrologist, the advanced Dialysis Machinery with a dedicated space is your one-stop solution to all your Dialysis Needs.

Cardiology

We are a tertiary care centre equipped with cutting edge technology to offer cardiac care program to our patients. Our hospitals are equipped with state of the art Cath Labs, operation theatres, and several other heart care technologies.
