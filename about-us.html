BRAND LEGACY

From its humble beginnings as a computer center in a two-room set up at Capri Trade Center, Graphic Era has grown into a prestigious educational organization with two universities spread over 4 campuses across two cities- working hand-in-hand to groom the youth of the nation into industry-ready professionals.

Graphic Era’s reputation for imparting quality education, pioneering research and discoveries, and training the next generation of leaders testifies to its unwavering commitment to the progress and welfare of society. The rich history and success of Graphic Era Group along with a strong philanthropic desire inspired Prof. (Dr.) Kamal Ghanshala, Chairman, Graphic Era Group to strengthen his mission to promote public welfare by setting up a state-of-the-art, super-specialty hospital.

Graphic Era Hospital/GEIMS aims to establish a world-class, patient-centric healthcare ecosystem in Uttarakhand & emerge as a center of excellence for quality healthcare and medical research in Uttarakhand and India.

Core Values

Efficiency

Patient service lies at the heart of our work. We strive to ensure that our medical knowledge and resources are optimally utilized to serve our patients in the most efficient manner.

Compassion

People who come to us can expect staff members who will treat you with respect, dignity and compassion and will walk an extra mile to ensure that you receive the care you deserve.

Excellence

This is the ultimate aim of all our efforts. We endeavor to ensure that all aspects of our systems reflect this value and our services surpass your expectations.

Consistency

We take great measures to make sure that highest level of patient care and satisfaction is met at every stage, every time. We believe that only through consistency, we can retain our patient’s trust and fulfill our goals.

Trust

Your health and safety is our topmost priority. We trust our exceptional caregivers to take care of you and your loved ones and their ability to provide them the highest standards of medical care

Accountability

Ethics and accountability are integral part of our culture. We take pride in our accomplishments and take full responsibility for our shortcomings so that we can serve you better.

Mission

To become the most trusted institution for world-class healthcare and medical education in the state of Uttarakhand.
  • Providing the highest quality patient care.
  • Establish state of the art infrastructure, clinical and nursing care expertise.
  • Provide quality medical education to nurture skilled Medical Practitioners.
  • Doing charity to humanity irrespective of caste, race, or denominations.

Vision

To bring affordable, quality patientcare within the reach of every individual and produce skilled, competent, and ethical medical practitioners.

Graphic Era Institute of Medical Sciences is the newest inclusion in the list of premier institutions under the umbrella of Graphic Era Group of Institutions.

This ultra modern facility being completed at a rapid pace, will finish with a 1250 Bed Capacity equipped with all state of the art equipment meeting all global standards, giving the people of the state and the rest of the country a world-class medical facility at an affordable price.
